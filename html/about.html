Services

- Vulberability Assessment: A high level scan using automated tools and passive information gathering techniques to find potential vulnerabilities.
- Penetration Testing: A complete evaluation with one or more specific target(s), this includes vulnerability assessment, except we attempt to probe and exploit further, simulating a real attack.
- Red Team Exercises: Long term engagement with more broad targets and secrecy, with a dedicated blue team in your company, simulating complete and unexpected attacks to improve your resilience.
