Vulnerability Assessment

  • Estimated Engagement Period: 2-5 business days.
  • Passive Information Gathering: Using google and other search tools, and all other public information available to increase attack surface.
  • Port and Service Scanning: Using nmap and other automated tools to scan for open ports and find services actively running, find potential public exploits and CVE's without applying them.
  • FREE Assessment Offer: For first time engagement with an organization, inquire about a free vulnerability scan!
  • Summary: A high level, non-invasive scan to get potential attack vectors and vulnerabilities for your organization, with report included.

Penetration Testing

  • Estimated Engagement Period: 1-4 weeks.
  • Scan, Exploit, Repeat: Attempt to apply all potential vulnerabilities found to gain further intel/access, repeat the process after further access is gained.
  • Applicable Areas: Internal, External, Web, Mobile, Wi-Fi, Physical, Cloud, and Hardware.
  • Communication: Since some public exploits have the risk of bringing down the service, any risky scripts will be checked with your IT Manager prior to running them.
  • Summary: A sophisticated and detailed cyber attack simulation with specific targets in mind, with a lot of communication and a detailed report.

Red Team Engagement

  • Estimated Engagement Period: 1-2 months or long term ongoing engagement.
  • Blue Team Required: Your organization will need dedicated individuals ready against any and all potential attacks from this exercise.
  • Broad Scope: The attack will be more general instead of specific targets, and the attack timing will not be communicated, simulating unexpected attacks.
  • Communication: More secrecy is involved during red team engagements to simulate unexpected attacks, a detailed report will follow after the exercise.
  • Summary: Similar to penetration testing, but with more secrecy and broad targets, as well as needing a dedicated blue team in your organization.

Phishing Campaign

  • Estimated Engagement Period: 1-8 weeks.
  • Testing the weakest link: People are generally the weakest link in cyber security, how well does your organization hold up against a sophisticated phishing campaign?
  • Various levels of difficulty: Phishing attempts can go from a poorly drafted email trying to give you "free" money, to highly convincing with prior information gathering.
  • Statistics and Advice: Find out how many attempts were successful, and additional advices/training on what to look out for in order to avoid them.
  • Summary: A test for people, the weakest link in cyber security with various difficulty levels.
