Red Team Services:

  • Penetration Testing: Simulate cyberattacks to identify vulnerabilities in an organization's systems, networks, applications, and infrastructure.
  • Social Engineering Assessments: Test employee susceptibility to manipulation through techniques like phishing, pretexting, and impersonation.
  • Zero-day Vulnerability Testing: Test defenses against unknown vulnerabilities.
  • Risk Assessment & Adversary Modeling: Analyze risks by modeling potential attackers and their likely strategies.
  • Red Team Exercises: Conduct complex simulations of advanced cyberattacks to test security preparedness and incident response capabilities.

Blue Team Services:

  • Security Monitoring & SIEM Setup: Implement SIEM systems to monitor network activity in real time, detecting and responding to threats.
  • Intrusion Detection & Prevention: Implement systems that detect and block unauthorized access attempts and potential breaches.
  • Incident Response Planning & Management: Develop detailed plans to respond to cyber incidents effectively, minimize damage, and recover quickly.
  • Security Awareness Training: Train employees to recognize and respond to security threats, including phishing attacks and social engineering attempts.
  • Endpoint Security: Implement measures to secure individual devices (computers, smartphones, IoT devices) against malware, unauthorized access, and data leakage.

Other Tech Services:

  • Physical & Facility Security Assessments: Evaluate an organization's physical security measures, including access controls, surveillance, and facility security.
  • Network Segmentation: Divide networks into smaller segments to limit lateral movement for attackers and contain potential breaches.
  • Security Architecture Review: Evaluate an organization's security architecture to ensure it aligns with best practices and industry standards.
  • Cybersecurity Consultation: Provide ongoing advisory services to help individuals/organizations develop and maintain effective cybersecurity strategies.
  • IT Automation and Coding: Develop scripts and automation for IT and other business processes.
